Our Process

Our Detailed Process

Having Symptoms?

Contact E-Health Via Phone, Email or fill out our get in touch form to schedule a consultation

Contact Us

Talk to our staff

After contacting E-Health we will evaluate the severity of your symptoms and direct you to one of the personalized plans we offer.

Option 1: One time doctor visit

With one time visits, E-Health gives you the option to choose a doctor of your choice and discuss with them one on one

Option 2: Subscription based visits

This option will be meant for conditions that require frequent checkups. Choice of doctor, or recommended doctor, will be provided and treatment will be included.

Option 3: Medication Refill/ Pick Up

If all you need is a refill or a pick up scheduled, don't worry. E-Health has doctors that can prescribe medication and partners that can deliver right to your doorstep.

Talk to one of our post visit experts

Our experts will let you know what the next steps are based on the feedback from the doctors and follow up with you regularly.

Get a chance to shop at our store

If you are in need of any materials for your treatment, we have you covered: from technology materials to fundamental medication.

Feedback Survey

We will email you a feedback survey that will ask you about your experience with E-Health, helping us enhance your next experience!

How it Works

  • We have the best doctors from around the world, working remotely
    • We allow you to book a consultation
    • We hold a preliminary consultation to get to know you
    • We match you with a doctor
    • We provide all materials necessary for the timebeing
    • You and the doctor consults as many times as necessary
    • You can drop feedback, request a new doctor, or purchase medicine
